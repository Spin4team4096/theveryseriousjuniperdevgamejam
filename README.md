# FLINGY.IO

**A match-based io game where you "Spin To Win":** 
- Spin to grow your flail, and fling it at others to kill them! 
- You each have 3 lives. Last player standing wins!

**PLAT AT: https://flingy.io**

### CONTROLS
- **Move your mouse** to control your player's direction
- **Spin your flail** by moving in circles to make it grow bigger
- **Left click** to release your flail and attack other players
- **I or F** to shows debug or fps/ping
